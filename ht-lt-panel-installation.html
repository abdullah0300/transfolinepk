04   Answers

Questions about panel installation.

Common questions about HT and LT panel services. Call us for anything else.

What is the difference between HT and LT panels?

HT (high-tension) panels handle high-voltage power (typically 11KV) coming from the utility grid. LT (low-tension) panels distribute the stepped-down power (typically 400V) to your equipment and circuits. Most installations need both.

Do you design custom panel layouts?

Yes. Every panel installation is designed to match your specific load distribution, protection requirements and site layout. We don't use generic off-the-shelf configurations for industrial or large commercial projects.

How long does panel installation take?

A typical panel installation takes 1–2 weeks from design approval to commissioning. Simple distribution board installations can be completed in 2–3 days.

Can you upgrade our existing panels?

Yes. We retrofit and upgrade outdated panels to meet current safety standards, improve load management and add capacity for future expansion. We assess your existing setup and recommend the most cost-effective upgrade path.
