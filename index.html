05   Answers

Questions, answered.

Filter by topic, or call us directly — we pick up.

BUYING What size transformer do I need for my factory?

Size depends on your load requirement. Share your monthly electricity bill and we'll calculate it for you — most factories run on 100–400 KVA.

BUYING Can I buy in bulk with a discount?

Yes. Bulk orders of 5+ units qualify for a 15–25% discount. Call for a custom quote tailored to your order.

BUYING Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes. Same-day within Lahore and 2–5 days to other cities. Shipping is included in your quoted price.

BUYING Can I order used transformers?

Yes. Every used unit is tested and certified — around 30% cheaper than new, with delivery in about 3 days.

REPAIR How fast is your repair service?

Most repairs are completed in 24–48 hours. Emergency repairs are available for a premium fee.

REPAIR Do you do on-site repairs?

Yes. For large units (500+ KVA) we come to your location. Smaller units can be brought to our workshop.

REPAIR What is oil dehydration and why is it needed?

It removes moisture from inside the transformer, extending its life by 5–10 years. We recommend it every 3–5 years.

TECH What are your transformer specifications?

25 KVA to 8000 KVA, oil-cooled and air-cooled options, ISO certified. Full specs are on each product page.

TECH Do you have spare parts in stock?

Yes. Common parts — gaskets, bushings, fuses — are always in stock. Rare parts are sourced within 48 hours.

TECH What safety certifications do you hold?

ISO certified, electrical board approved, and tested to IEC standards on every unit we supply.

PRICING What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, bank transfer and cheque. Custom orders require a 50% deposit; online payment is coming soon.

PRICING What is your return policy?

30-day returns on unused, unopened units. Warranty replacements are handled within 24 hours.
