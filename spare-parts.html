Questions about spare parts.

Common questions about transformer parts and supplies. Call us for anything else.

What transformer parts do you keep in stock?

We stock transformer oil, gaskets, seals, bushings, fuses, silica gel breathers, drain valves and temperature gauges. These cover the most common maintenance and repair needs for all transformer brands.

Can you source parts for older or rare transformer models?

Yes. Parts not in stock are sourced from our supplier network within 48 hours. Share your transformer details (brand, model, KVA) and we will find the right part.

Do you sell transformer oil in bulk?

Yes. We supply high-grade insulating oil in bulk quantities with quality certification. Contact us for bulk pricing based on the volume you need.

How do I know which parts are compatible with my transformer?

Tell us your transformer brand, model number and KVA rating. Our team will identify the exact compatible parts — no guesswork, no wrong orders.
