    Site survey & design

  4. 04 Week 6–8

04   Answers

Questions about electrification projects.

Common questions about town and society electrification. Call us for anything else.

What does a turnkey electrification project include?

Everything needed to bring power to your development — transformers, HT/LT panels, distribution wiring, poles, metering and grid connection. We handle design, procurement, installation, approvals and commissioning.

How long does a society electrification project take?

Most projects are completed within 4–8 weeks depending on the size of the development and regulatory approvals. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do you handle WAPDA/DISCO approvals?

Yes. We manage all regulatory paperwork, inspections and approvals required for grid connection. You don't need to coordinate with the utility company separately.

What areas do you serve for electrification projects?

We serve all major cities in Pakistan including Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, Faisalabad and Peshawar. Contact us for projects in other locations — we assess availability on a case-by-case basis.
