04   Answers

Questions about transformer repair.

Common questions about our repair services. Call us for anything else.

How fast can you repair my transformer?

Most repairs are completed in 24–48 hours. Emergency repairs — where downtime is costing you — are prioritised and often completed same day. Call us to discuss your timeline.

Do you repair transformers on-site?

Yes. For large units (500+ KVA) we send our mobile repair team to your location. Smaller units can be transported to our workshop in Lahore for faster turnaround.

What types of transformer faults do you repair?

We handle all common faults — winding damage, oil leaks, overheating, insulation breakdown, bushing failure, cooling system issues and electrical faults. If it's a transformer, we can fix it.

Is there a warranty on repair work?

Yes. Every repair is backed by a service warranty. If the same fault reoccurs within the warranty period, we repair it again at no additional cost.
